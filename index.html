How does geareye work

GearEye is a location-based logbook for high end gear. It uses super thin, smart RFID stickers that are easily tagged onto items so you never lose or forget anything. It's a photographer's best friend, a complete gear management solution. The stickers are super-affordable so you can put them on everything, and they'll always work because they don't run on batteries.

Your gear is your toolset. Stop risking your livelihood every day at work. Let your mind focus on the work, let GearEye focus on the gear.
